What is IPM?

IPM (Integrated Programme in Management) is a 5 year dual-degree course (BBA+MBA) offered currently by five IIMs to join and experience an IIM right after 12th. The course was first started by IIM Indore in 2011 and was followed along by IIM Rohtak in 2019. Also,

Which IIMs offer IPM?

Currently, 5 IIMs offer the IPM course: IIM Indore, IIM Rohtak, IIM Ranchi, IIM Bodhgaya and IIM Jammu. IIM Amritsar is reportedly planning to start their program in 2023.

Apart from IIMs, IIFT Kokinanda, NALSAR and Nirma University offer the IPM program too.

What is the exam syllabus?

IIM Indore‚Äôs IPMAT consists of 3 sections: Quantitative Ability (MCQ) Quantitative Ability (SA) & Verbal Ability (MCQ). The analysis of IPMAT Indore past year paper can be found here.

On the contrary, IIM Rohtak‚Äôs IPMAT consists of 3 MCQ based sections: Quantitative Ability, Verbal Ability & Logical Reasoning.

How many seats are there for the IPM?

There are 660 seats across IIMs for the IPM program.
IIM Indore = 150 seats
IIM Rohtak = 210 seats
IIM Ranchi = 120 seats
IIM Bodhgaya = 120 seats
IIM Jammu = 60 seats

Can a non-math student clear IPMAT?

While having a math background puts you at an advantage, with the right amount of practice and hardwork anyone can clear the IPMAT- even non-math students. Many non-math students are already studying in the IPM course.
